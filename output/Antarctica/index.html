Antarctica

Antarctica clues

  • Language:

    No indigenous languages; scientific research predominantly conducted in English.

  • Cars:

    No vehicle registration plates; vehicles are often specialized for harsh conditions.

  • Driving:

    No standard roadways; vehicles operate in research stations and bases.

  • Signs:

    Minimal signage; any signs present are related to research activities or safety warnings.

  • Environment:

    Ice-covered landscape, glaciers, snowfields, and mountain ranges; no vegetation.

  • Infrastructure:

    Research stations with basic facilities; often prefabricated buildings designed for extreme weather.

  • Culture:

    Presence of international research stations; visitors may see flags of various countries.

  • Bonus:

    Limited human presence; typical features include snowmobiles, scientific equipment, and weather monitoring stations.

  • Flag:

    No official flag for Antarctica; various countries' flags may be seen at research stations.
