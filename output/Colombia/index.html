Colombia

Colombia clues

  • Language:

    Spanish is the official language, using the Latin alphabet with no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a yellow band at the top, featuring the province's name.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are usually in Spanish, with clear and colorful designs, often including symbols for warnings and directions.

  • Environment:

    Colombia has a diverse landscape featuring mountains (Andes), jungles (Amazon), and coastal areas (Caribbean and Pacific).

  • Culture:

    Look for markers such as street vendors selling fruit, colorful murals, and public plazas often filled with people.

  • Bonus:

    Street View may show unique features like coffee plantations in the countryside or vibrant city life in urban areas.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three horizontal stripes: yellow (top, half of the flag), blue, and red.
