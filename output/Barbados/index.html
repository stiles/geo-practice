Barbados

Barbados clues

  • Language:

    English; uses the Latin alphabet without special characters

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top; format typically includes a combination of letters and numbers

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the left side of the road

  • Signs:

    Road signs are mostly in English; they are similar to those used in the UK, with some unique local variations

  • Environment:

    Tropical climate with white sandy beaches, palm trees, and lush vegetation; the island has coral limestone and hilly terrain

  • Infrastructure:

    Colonial architecture is prevalent, featuring colorful buildings, wooden shutters, and gabled roofs; urban areas have a mix of modern and traditional designs

  • Culture:

    Influences from African, British, and Indian cultures; look for local festivals, music, and food markets; rum distilleries are common

  • Bonus:

    Look for distinctive roundabouts with local art, vibrant street art, and the presence of local flora like hibiscus and bougainvillea

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three vertical bands of ultramarine, gold, and ultramarine, with a black trident head in the center
