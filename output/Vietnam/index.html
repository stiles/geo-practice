Vietnam

Vietnam clues

  • Language:

    Vietnamese; uses the Latin alphabet with diacritics (e.g., ă, â, ê, ô, ơ, ư)

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue border; the format typically includes numbers and letters indicating the province.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are often in Vietnamese, with some international symbols; signs may use bright colors and are usually well-maintained.

  • Environment:

    Diverse landscapes, including rice paddies, mountains, and coastal areas; tropical climate in the south and temperate conditions in the north.

  • Infrastructure:

    A mix of modern high-rises and traditional architecture, including colonial-era buildings; often visible street vendors and motorbike traffic.

  • Culture:

    Presence of street food stalls, traditional markets, and the conical hat (nón lá); unique cultural icons like the lotus flower.

  • Bonus:

    Distinctive motorbike culture with heavy traffic; presence of local language on storefronts and advertisements.

  • Flag:

    A red background with a large yellow star in the center.
