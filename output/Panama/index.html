Panama

Panama clues

  • Language:

    Spanish is the official language, using the Latin alphabet with common Spanish diacritics.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue stripe at the top; the format usually includes a combination of letters and numbers.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are primarily in Spanish and often feature the distinctive use of symbols and colors typical of Central America.

  • Environment:

    Panama features a mix of tropical rainforest, coastal areas, and mountainous regions; notable locations include the Panama Canal and lush greenery.

  • Infrastructure:

    Urban areas showcase a blend of colonial architecture and modern skyscrapers, especially in Panama City with its distinctive skyline.

  • Culture:

    Look for traditional Panamanian elements like Pollera dresses, indigenous art, and festive decorations during local celebrations.

  • Bonus:

    The presence of the Panama Canal, tropical flora, and the common sight of indigenous people in traditional attire can also help identify the country.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of four quadrants: a blue star on a white background, a red star on a white background, a blue field, and a red field.
