São Tomé and Príncipe

São Tomé and Príncipe clues

  • Language:

    Portuguese; uses the Latin alphabet with standard characters

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top, displaying the letters 'STP' for São Tomé and Príncipe.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are usually in Portuguese, with standard white and blue colors; some may include pictograms.

  • Environment:

    The landscape is characterized by lush greenery, volcanic mountains, and a tropical climate; there are often coastal views and beaches.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architectural styles include colonial Portuguese buildings, simple concrete structures, and colorful houses; urban areas may show a mix of modern and traditional designs.

  • Culture:

    Look for local markets, traditional fishing boats, and street vendors selling food; music and art reflecting African and Portuguese influences are also common.

  • Bonus:

    Distinctive features include the prevalence of palm trees, the presence of the São Tomé and Príncipe currency (Dobra), and local fauna like giant tortoises.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three horizontal stripes of green, yellow, and red, with a black triangle on the left side containing a yellow star.
