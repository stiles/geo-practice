Comoros

Comoros clues

  • Language:

    The official languages are Comorian, Arabic, and French. The Comorian language uses a Latin-based alphabet with some Arabic influences.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates typically have a white background with a blue band at the top featuring the country code 'KM'.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are predominantly in French, with some in Arabic, and are often bilingual. Signs may include pictograms to aid understanding.

  • Environment:

    Comoros is characterized by volcanic islands, lush greenery, and beautiful beaches. There are mountainous terrains and a tropical climate.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architectural styles reflect a mix of Arab, African, and French influences, with traditional wooden houses and modern concrete buildings.

  • Culture:

    Distinctive cultural markers include local markets, traditional clothing, and mosques. Look for signs of a vibrant community life.

  • Bonus:

    Street scenes may include palm trees, local fruit vendors, and unique patterns of traffic that reflect the island lifestyle.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of four horizontal stripes (green, white, red, and blue) with a white crescent and four white stars on the left side.
