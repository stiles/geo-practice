South Georgia

South Georgia clues

  • Language:

    English is the primary language, with no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicles do not have standard registration plates; most vehicles are government-owned.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are minimal and often feature English text; few official road signs exist.

  • Environment:

    The landscape consists of rugged mountains, glaciers, and a cold, maritime climate; often features a tundra-like environment.

  • Infrastructure:

    Limited infrastructure; primarily research stations and a few buildings with utilitarian architecture.

  • Culture:

    Predominantly British heritage; sites may include historical whaling stations and research facilities.

  • Bonus:

    Look for the presence of wildlife such as penguins and seals; very few human-made structures.

  • Flag:

    The flag features a blue field with the Union Jack in the canton and the coat of arms of South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands on the fly side.
