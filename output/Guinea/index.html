Guinea

Guinea clues

  • Language:

    French (official), Malinke, Susu, Fulfulde; uses the Latin alphabet

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top; format includes the letters 'GN' for Guinea

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road

  • Signs:

    Road signs are predominantly in French, with some using local languages; generally rectangular with a mix of colors

  • Environment:

    Characterized by hilly terrain, forests, and rivers; coastal areas near the Atlantic Ocean

  • Infrastructure:

    Urban areas feature a mix of colonial and modern architecture; rural areas may have thatched-roof houses

  • Culture:

    Presence of vibrant markets, traditional crafts, and cultural festivals; visible use of traditional clothing

  • Bonus:

    Street View may show lush greenery, bustling markets, and a variety of transportation methods including bicycles and motorbikes

  • Flag:

    Vertical stripes of red, yellow, and green from left to right
