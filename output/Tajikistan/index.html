Tajikistan

Tajikistan clues

  • Language:

    Tajik (written in Cyrillic script), Russian is also widely spoken.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are typically white with blue and green accents, featuring the letters 'TJ' for Tajikistan.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are mostly in Tajik and Russian, with Cyrillic script. Signs may be colorful and include both street names and directional information.

  • Environment:

    Tajikistan features mountainous terrain, with the Pamir Mountains being prominent. The landscape includes valleys, rivers, and some arid areas.

  • Infrastructure:

    Urban areas may show Soviet-era architecture, with flat-roofed buildings and a mix of modern constructions. Rural areas have simpler, often clay-brick houses.

  • Culture:

    Look for traditional elements such as yurt-like structures, local markets, and signs of Persian cultural influence, such as intricate tile work.

  • Bonus:

    Presence of mountainous roads, occasional livestock on the road, and the use of both Tajik and Russian in public spaces.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three horizontal stripes: red at the top, white in the middle, and green at the bottom, with a golden crown and seven stars in the center.
