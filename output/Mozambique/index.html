Mozambique

Mozambique clues

  • Language:

    Portuguese is the official language, using the Latin alphabet with no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates typically feature a white background with blue and yellow stripes. The format often includes a combination of letters and numbers.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are generally in Portuguese and may include both metric and imperial measurements. Signs are usually rectangular with colorful backgrounds.

  • Environment:

    The landscape features a mix of coastal areas, savannas, and plateaus. There are also significant natural features like the Zambezi River and numerous national parks.

  • Infrastructure:

    Urban areas may display colonial architecture with Portuguese influences, while rural areas often have simpler structures. Many buildings may be made of brick or concrete.

  • Culture:

    Common cultural markers include vibrant markets, traditional music, and local crafts. Look for signs of Portuguese influence in food and architecture.

  • Bonus:

    Look for the presence of palm trees, tropical plants, and the occasional sighting of wildlife or open savannah.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three horizontal stripes of green, black, and yellow, with a red triangle on the left featuring a crossed hoe and AK-47 over a book.
