Singapore

Singapore clues

  • Language:

    English is the primary language, with Malay, Mandarin, and Tamil also widely spoken; the alphabet is Latin with no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a black font; the format usually consists of one letter followed by a number and two letters (e.g., S1234AB).

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are in English and follow international standards; they typically use clear symbols and are well-maintained.

  • Environment:

    The landscape is predominantly urban with high-rise buildings; there are also parks and green spaces, and the coastal area features beaches.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architectural styles range from modern high-rises to colonial buildings; notable structures include Marina Bay Sands and Gardens by the Bay.

  • Culture:

    Distinctive markers include hawker centers, a mix of cultural festivals, and public transport systems like the MRT.

  • Bonus:

    Taxis are often brightly colored; there are extensive pedestrian walkways and covered walkways connecting buildings.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of a red field with a white crescent moon and five white stars in the top left corner.
