Brazil

Brazil clues

  • Language:

    Portuguese, using the Latin alphabet with diacritics such as ç, ã, and é

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates have a white background with a blue band at the top featuring the name of the state; plates also include a QR code and a unique alphanumeric code.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are typically in Portuguese and feature a mix of standard international symbols and unique local signage, often with a green and yellow color scheme.

  • Environment:

    Diverse geographical features including the Amazon rainforest, coastal beaches, mountains, and urban areas with a tropical climate.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architectural styles range from colonial buildings to modern skyscrapers, with notable use of colorful facades in cities like Salvador and Brasília's modernist architecture.

  • Culture:

    Indicators of Brazilian culture include samba and carnival influences, street art, and local cuisine represented in food stalls and restaurants.

  • Bonus:

    Look for the presence of tropical vegetation, favelas (informal housing), and occasional Portuguese-language advertising.

  • Flag:

    A green field with a yellow diamond in the center containing a blue globe with 27 white stars arranged as the night sky over Rio de Janeiro and a white band with the motto 'Ordem e Progresso'.
