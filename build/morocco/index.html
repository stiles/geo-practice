Morocco

Morocco clues

  • Language:

    Arabic (official), Berber (official), French (widely spoken); Arabic script used

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top containing the region code; the license plate format is usually a mix of letters and numbers

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road

  • Signs:

    Road signs are primarily in Arabic and French, with distinctive green and white colors; highway signs often feature symbols and pictograms

  • Environment:

    Diverse landscapes including mountains (Atlas and Rif), deserts (Sahara), and coastal areas along the Atlantic and Mediterranean; vegetation varies from lush areas to arid regions

  • Infrastructure:

    Mix of modern and traditional architecture; notable features include riads (traditional Moroccan houses), mosques with minarets, and medinas in cities

  • Culture:

    Presence of traditional markets (souks), street vendors, and distinct Moroccan motifs such as tile work and ornate doorways; vibrant colors in urban areas

  • Bonus:

    Distinctive yellow taxis in urban areas, and the occasional sight of camels or traditional Berber tents in rural areas

  • Flag:

    Red background with a green pentagram (five-pointed star) in the center
