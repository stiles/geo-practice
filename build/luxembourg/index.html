Luxembourg

Luxembourg clues

  • Language:

    Luxembourgish, French, German; uses the Latin alphabet with special characters like 'é', 'ë', and 'ä'.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue strip on the left featuring the letter 'L' for Luxembourg.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are typically in French or Luxembourgish, with clear, standardized symbols; often bilingual.

  • Environment:

    Features rolling hills, forests, and rivers; urban areas are mixed with green spaces.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architecture includes a mix of medieval structures, modern buildings, and European-style town squares; cobblestone streets in older areas.

  • Culture:

    Presence of castles, fortifications, and a blend of French, German, and Luxembourgish cultural elements.

  • Bonus:

    Look for the distinct green and red markings on some public buildings and the use of Luxembourgish in local signage.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three horizontal stripes: red, white, and light blue.
