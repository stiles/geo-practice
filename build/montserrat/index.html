Montserrat clues

  • Language:

    English is the official language, with a standard Latin alphabet and no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band on the left side; they often feature the coat of arms of Montserrat.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are primarily in English, following the British style, with clear typography and often using reflective materials.

  • Bonus:

    Look for the presence of English-style telephone boxes, tropical flora, and occasional views of the volcanic landscape.

    The flag consists of a blue ensign with the Union Jack in the canton and the coat of arms of Montserrat on the fly side.
