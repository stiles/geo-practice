Yemen

Yemen clues

  • Language:

    Arabic is the official language. The Arabic script is used, which includes letters like أ, ب, ت, ث, ج.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are typically white with black text. They may include Arabic numerals and letters.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are primarily in Arabic, often with distinctive shapes and colors. Some may have English translations.

  • Environment:

    Yemen features a diverse landscape including mountainous regions, deserts, and coastal areas along the Red Sea and Gulf of Aden.

  • Infrastructure:

    Traditional architecture includes mud-brick buildings, especially in cities like Sana'a, which have unique tower houses and intricate decorations.

  • Culture:

    Look for traditional markets (souks), mosques, and cultural attire such as the ‘thobe’ and ‘jambia’ (dagger) worn by men.

  • Bonus:

    Street View may show unique features like the ancient city of Shibam, known for its skyscraper-like mud buildings, or terraced farming in rural areas.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three horizontal stripes: red at the top, white in the middle, and black at the bottom.
