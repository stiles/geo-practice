Macau

Macau clues

  • Language:

    Cantonese is commonly spoken; Traditional Chinese characters are used.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue border.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are primarily in Chinese and Portuguese, with some English; signs are often bilingual.

  • Environment:

    Macau features a mix of urban landscapes with a coastal environment; hilly terrain is common.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architectural styles include a blend of Portuguese colonial and modern skyscrapers; notable structures include the Ruins of St. Paul's and the Macau Tower.

  • Culture:

    Macau is known for its casinos, unique fusion of Portuguese and Chinese cultures, and traditional festivals.

  • Bonus:

    Look for unique features like the presence of gambling establishments and the distinct Portuguese tiles on buildings.

  • Flag:

    The flag of Macau is green with a lotus flower above a stylized bridge and two waves, representing the region's cultural heritage.
