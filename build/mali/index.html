Mali

Mali clues

  • Language:

    French is the official language. The alphabet is Latin with no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are typically white with a blue band on the left featuring the country code 'ML'.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are generally in French, using standard European signage styles with some local variations.

  • Environment:

    Mali features a mix of savannah, desert, and river landscapes, including the Niger River and the Sahara Desert.

  • Infrastructure:

    Urban areas may feature mud-brick buildings and traditional architecture, while cities like Bamako have a mix of modern and colonial influences.

  • Culture:

    Distinctive markers include traditional markets, mosques, and cultural events such as festivals featuring music and dance.

  • Bonus:

    Look for unique street vendors, traditional attire, and the presence of bicycles and motorcycles as common modes of transport.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three vertical stripes of red, yellow, and green, from left to right.
