Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha clues

  • Language:

    English is the common language. The alphabet is Latin with no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue strip on the left featuring the coat of arms.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are primarily in English and often feature a mix of British and local styles.

  • Environment:

    The landscape includes rugged terrain, volcanic islands, and lush greenery, particularly on Saint Helena.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architectural styles vary from colonial buildings in Jamestown, Saint Helena to simple structures on Tristan da Cunha.

  • Culture:

    Distinctive markers include the local flora and fauna, as well as traditional fishing and farming practices.

  • Bonus:

    The presence of unique wildlife, such as the Saint Helena plover, and the lack of large urban areas are notable.

  • Flag:

    The flag features a blue field with the Union Jack in the canton and the coat of arms of Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha on the fly side.
