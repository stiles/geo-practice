Monaco clues

  • Language:

    French is the official language, using the standard Latin alphabet with typical French diacritics.

  • Cars:

    Vehicles have white registration plates with a blue band on the left side displaying 'MC' for Monaco.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are in French and follow the standard European road sign conventions.
