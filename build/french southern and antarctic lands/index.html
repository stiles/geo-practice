    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are primarily in French, often with a blue background and white lettering, following French road sign conventions.

    The landscape comprises sub-Antarctic islands with rugged coastlines, volcanic terrain, and a mix of tundra and marine environments.

  • Infrastructure:

    Limited urban infrastructure, mainly research stations and small settlements with a functional architectural style, often made from durable materials to withstand harsh weather.

    Cultural markers include French-style architecture, local wildlife, and references to scientific research, as the area is primarily used for scientific exploration.
