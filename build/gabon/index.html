Gabon

Gabon clues

  • Language:

    French is the official language. The alphabet is Latin-based with no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are typically white with a blue band at the top featuring the country code 'GAB'.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are often in French and follow international standards, with a mix of metric measurements.

  • Environment:

    Gabon features dense tropical rainforest, rivers, and a coastline along the Atlantic Ocean. The landscape is generally hilly.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architectural styles include a mix of modern buildings and traditional structures, with some influence from colonial architecture.

  • Culture:

    Look for vibrant markets, traditional crafts, and cultural festivals that showcase Gabon's rich heritage.

  • Bonus:

    Street View may show wildlife, lush greenery, and scenes from national parks, as Gabon is known for its biodiversity.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three horizontal stripes: green (top), yellow (middle), and blue (bottom).
