Niger

Niger clues

  • Language:

    French is the official language, and the alphabet is Latin with no special characters.

  • Cars:

    Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top; they usually feature the letters 'NE' for Niger, followed by a numerical code.

  • Driving:

    Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

  • Signs:

    Road signs are predominantly in French, with a mix of local languages; signs are usually rectangular with clear, bold lettering.

  • Environment:

    Niger features vast desert landscapes, particularly the Sahara, alongside savannah and some mountainous regions in the Aïr Mountains.

  • Infrastructure:

    Architecture includes traditional mud-brick houses (terre battue), modern buildings in cities like Niamey, and distinctively designed markets.

  • Culture:

    Look for traditional attire such as the boubou, vibrant markets, and cultural events like the Cure Salée festival.

  • Bonus:

    Desert landscapes, signs in French, and local markets with a bustling atmosphere can be distinctive features.

  • Flag:

    The flag consists of three horizontal stripes: orange, white, and green, with a central orange circle.
